Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How does your AI-powered development work?

Our AI-powered development platform analyzes your requirements and automates repetitive coding tasks. This allows our developers to focus on creating unique features that make your product stand out. The result is faster development without sacrificing quality or customization.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We implement enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure authentication, and regular security audits. Your data is stored in compliance with global privacy standards, and we sign comprehensive NDAs before starting any project.

How long does it take to build an MVP?

Most MVPs can be completed within 30 days. The exact timeline depends on the complexity of your requirements, but our AI-powered development process significantly accelerates the build time compared to traditional development approaches. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

What technologies do you use?

We work with modern tech stacks including React, Vue, Angular for frontend; Node.js, Python, Ruby for backend; and AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure for hosting. We select the best technologies for your specific project needs and can adapt to your preferred stack if you have one.

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy. Simply book a free consultation call using our online calendar. During this call, we'll discuss your project requirements, answer any questions you have, and outline the next steps. After the call, we'll send you a detailed proposal with timeline and pricing.
